Services

Car electronics:

Car electronics are an essential part of your car, even if you do not drive electrically. The electronics system not only ensures that your lamps are lit and the radio can play, but also supplies the starter motor with energy. With broken electronics, the engine will not start, so you cannot drive. Make sure to stop by so that we can check the state of your car

Automotive mechanics:

We repair cars and perform maintenance work to keep them roadworthy and to prevent major repair costs for our customers, investigate different systems in cars to diagnose problems and perform automated diagnostic tests to help them identify components that may not work properly. We remove parts that are worn or not working properly and replace them with new or used parts. We perform routine maintenance, such as oil, filter and belt changes according to schedules prepared by different car manufacturers. With all the necessary machines in our possession, we will have no trouble solving all sorts of problems with the mechanics of your car
